Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking with us.

How early should I book your services?
We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large corporate events to ensure availability and ample planning time.
Do you provide decoration services separately?
Yes! While we offer full-service packages, we are also happy to provide standalone decoration or catering services depending on your needs.
Can I customize the package?
Absolutely. All our packages serve as a starting point. We specialize in custom-tailored solutions to match your exact vision.
What is your cancellation policy?
Deposits are generally non-refundable. Cancellations made 30 days prior to the event may be eligible for a partial refund or rescheduling depending on the circumstances.
